== Description ==

Crowley is a WordPress theme that is designed for bloggers and online magazines.

The theme is completely customizable through the Customizer and allows you to build almost any type of online blog. Its style is clean and mininal, just perfect to keep your content and media organized and searchable. The sidebar is customizable as well through widgets, and also archive pages' appearance can be modified, allowing a one or more columns layout.

Upload your custom logo, and build the design you have in mind, with the ability to customize colors as well.

Lightweight, completely responsive and SEO friendly, Crowley is the perfect theme for a dynamic blog, that is clean and fast: start publishing your articles in a simple and intuitive way!

== Installation ==

1. In your admin panel, go to Appearance -> Themes and click the 'Add New' button.
2. Type in Crowley in the search form and press the 'Enter' key on your keyboard.
3. Click on the 'Activate' button to use your new theme right away.
4. Navigate to Appearance > Customize in your admin panel and customize to taste.
