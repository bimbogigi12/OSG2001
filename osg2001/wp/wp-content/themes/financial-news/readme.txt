==================================
Description
==================================

The Financial News WordPress theme by TodayTrader.com is ideal for editorial websites like dynamic news sites, modern blogs and online magazines with topics related to business, economy, stock market, professional trading, investing or anything else. This news theme includes a fully widgetized Homepage template which you can use to easily build your front page layout based on widgets with drag & drop. You can use the included custom widgets to display a lot of content and news stories about stocks, trading ideas, economic events, business news or other editorial content related to the financial industry.
